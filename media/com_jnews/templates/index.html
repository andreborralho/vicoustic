  First Product
Wednesday, 13th February 2013
The Visquare Pro was a custom panel made especially for the BBC last year and that has now been added to the standard line up of products.

While the Visquare has centered perforations, the Pro version has equally distanced perforations on the entire panel so that when placed in a sequence, you cannot detect when one panel ends and the other starts.
The continuity effect is ideal to treat larger surfaces. It comes in all the usual finishes (M1, Premium, Tech, Tech with Black/White veil, Tech Premium).
